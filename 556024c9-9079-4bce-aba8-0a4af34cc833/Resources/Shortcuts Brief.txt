Shortcut brief help :

F1 - this help in chatbox
F2 - Ready all your cards
F10 - Reset Screen Position
F11 - Full Screen
CTRL+SHIFT+R - Flip a coin
tab - Pass
Double click on a card - Commit/Ready card
CTRL+M - Mulligan your hand
CTRL+D - Draw card
CTRL+SHIFT+D - Draw many cards
CTRL+S - Shuffle Deck
CTRL+C - Choose card(s) from top X of your Deck, shuffle the rest and put to the bottom of your Deck
CTRL+SHIFT+C - Scry x cards
CTRL+W - Add Prestige
CTRL+SHIFT+W - Remove Prestige
CTRL+G - Add Gold
CTRL+SHIFT+G - Remove Gold
While hovering on a card :
If Card is a banner : CTRL+SHIFT+U - Set up
If Card is a character : CTRL+SHIFT+A - Declare attack
CTRL+X - Attach Card to Target/Dettach
CTRL+F - Flip card
CTRL+A - Add/Remove Marker
Del - Discard card
CTRL+Q - Add random Marker
CTRL+W - Remove random Marker
CTRL+H - Move to Hand
CTRL+E - Move to Deck
ALT+CTRL+E - Move to Deck (bottom)
PgUp - Bring to Front
PgDn - Send to Back
